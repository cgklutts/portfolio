The Golden Stache

Standing 18 inches tall, this meticulously crafted bust was 3D-printed in seven separate pieces and expertly joined using precision alignment keys. Each joint was bonded, sanded, and painted multiple times to achieve a flawless, seamless finish. Custom internal weights lend the piece a substantial, metallic presence, while the antique gold rub and buff bring a timeless, classical elegance.
