The Golden Ledger

Standing at 18 inches tall, this bust was meticulously crafted from a mere photo. Modelled & then 3D-printed in seven separate pieces and expertly joined using precision alignment keys. Each joint was bonded, sanded, and painted multiple times to achieve a flawless, seamless finish. Custom internal weights lend the piece a substantial, metallic presence, while the antique gold rub and buff bring a timeless, classical elegance. Sealed with a glossy polyurethane coating, this fusion of modern fabrication and traditional artistry culminates in a striking statement piece‚Äîpresented to an executive of Wall Street.
